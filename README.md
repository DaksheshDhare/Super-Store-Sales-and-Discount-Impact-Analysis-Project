# Superstore Sales & Discount Impact Analysis

An exploratory data analysis project based on the Superstore retail dataset.  
The objective was to analyze sales performance, profitability, and the effect of discount strategies on business outcomes using dashboards built in Power BI and Excel.

---

## Project Goal

Retail businesses often offer discounts to increase sales, but excessive discounts can reduce profitability.

This project explores:

• Sales and profit performance over time  
• Product categories driving the most revenue  
• Cities contributing the highest profit  
• How discount levels influence profitability  

The analysis converts raw sales data into interactive dashboards that highlight important business insights.

---

## Tools & Technologies

**Data Preparation**
- Power Query for cleaning and transforming the dataset

**Visualization & Analysis**
- Microsoft Power BI for interactive dashboards
- Microsoft Excel for pivot-based analysis and dashboarding

---

## Dashboard Analysis

### Sales Performance
- Total sales, profit, and quantity KPIs
- Monthly sales and profit trends
- State-level comparison of sales and profit

### Product Insights
- Category-wise sales distribution
- Top profitable products
- Sub-category performance comparison

### Geographic Insights
- City-level profit contribution
- Regional performance analysis

### Discount Impact
- Analysis of discount levels and profitability
- Identification of products affected by high discounts

---

## Key Insights

- Technology and Office Supplies categories contribute significantly to sales.
- Certain cities generate a large share of total profit.
- High discounts often reduce profit margins.
- A small number of products drive a large portion of overall profit.
